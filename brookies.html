Brookies

Description

These Brookies are rich and tasty bars made with half brownie and half chocolate chip cookie. Easy to make with basic ingredients, this is the best way to enjoy your two favorite sweet treats in one!

Ingredients

  • sugar
  • butter
  • brown sugar
  • baking soda
  • eggs
  • salt
  • chocolate chips
  • baking powder
  • cocoa powder
  • all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Melt the butter and blend in the cocoa powder
  2. add both sugars, vanilla and eggs and agitate until combined
  3. Fold in the flour then mix in the chocolate chips
  4. Transfer the brownie batter in the prepared baking pan and smooth it out
  5. Mix the butter and sugar until fluffy then add the vanilla, eggs, salt, baking soda, and powder
  6. Fold in the flour and add chocolate chips until combined.
  7. Add dollops of cookie dough on top of the brownie batter, cover with foil then bake
